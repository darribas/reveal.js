So what does Dani Arribas-Bel do?

I am interested in how the computational advances of our age, combined with statistical methodologies that explicitly include space, can further our understanding of cities in general and their spatial structure in particular.

I am interested in the intersection between:

  • Cities

    • Employment distribution, population density...
    • Can we measure these spatial processes? How? Causes? Consequences?
    • [Plan] What do they tell us about the part of the city we can't measure?

  • Computers

    • Methods: ESDA, spatial econometrics, machine learning
    • Code: implementation in open-source libraries
    • Hardware: super-computers, [Plan] sensors

  • Data

    • New available sources of (geo-)data

... and fancy maps!
